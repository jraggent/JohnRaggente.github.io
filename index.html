All started with a molecule named DNA

My name is John Raggente. I'm a curious individual who is always trying to understand the meaning behind things. Learning that a small molecule called "DNA" is the driver behind life itself I decided to study and understand it as much as I could. This lead me to pursue a Bachelor's in Molecular Biology and Genetics and the University of Guelph. Throughout my undergrad, I have learned an immense amount more about DNA and have unofficially specialized in plant genetics. Doing research on improving crop plants is of high interest to me and part of what has led me down this path.

Outside of school I exercise regularly, have my own projects, and work part-time. Scientific studies require a level of understanding with statistical analysis tools which originally got me interested in learning SQL, R, and Tableau since they would improve the quality of my academic work. I've also completed the "Data Analytics Certificatation" offered by google consisting of 8 courses that fully teach anyone how to conduct data analysis.
